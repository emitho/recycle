About Re-cycle

Stavanger is a fantastic coastal city sprawling with life, contrasting its unique nature which is oddly always just a stone's throw away. Therefore we couldn't believe that there weren't a sustainable alternative to all the cruises coming and going in the city harbour.

Our goal is to focus on the real treasures of the region, by offering affordable tour packages for both singles, couples, families and groups.

Let us take you through the majestic forests, the vast farmlands, and the unforgettable sunsets by the sea. Our tour guides are locals who love to travel themselves, and with a lot of experience from tourism industries. If anybody knows anything about their stuff, it's these guys. Let them share their love for our fantastic region.

And let's do it sustainably.
